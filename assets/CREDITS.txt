The slideshow shows a single static hero. To add a cycling slideshow, save
additional photos as hero-N.jpg in this folder and add matching <div
class="hero-slide" data-slide="N" ...> lines in index.html. Landscape
orientation works best; 2400px wide or wider preserves detail at large
screen sizes.
